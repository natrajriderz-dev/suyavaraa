THE FISH TRAP — AI SAFETY SYSTEM🪤  FISH TRAP AIThe world's first proactive scammer interception system for dating appsThe Concept — How It WorksInstead of waiting for scammers to harm real users and then banning them, BOND intercepts them BEFORE they ever reach a real person. We deploy 20 volunteer decoy profiles as bait. Unverified new users can ONLY interact with these decoy profiles — not real users. Behind every decoy profile, our Safety AI chats as a real person would, probing for scammer and predator behavior patterns.Why This Is Impossible to Copy QuicklyEvery other app waits for reports. We hunt proactively. Our AI is trained on thousands of scammer scripts, grooming patterns, and predator conversation flows. We know exactly what a scammer does — and we bait them into revealing themselves before they can harm anyone.Fish Trap — Step by Step Flow1New User RegistersUser downloads BOND and creates account with basic details. No verification yet.2Quarantine Zone ActivatedUnverified user enters QUARANTINE — they see a limited feed. They can only like or message the 20 decoy profiles. Real user profiles are invisible to them.3AI Takes Over Decoy ProfilesWhen unverified user messages a decoy, our Safety AI responds naturally — like a real person. AI continues conversation, probing behavioral patterns.4Behavioral Analysis RunningAI analyzes: speed of conversation, language patterns, requests made, links shared, money mention, phone number requests, emotional manipulation tactics, copy-paste script detection.5Trust Score CalculatedBased on conversation AI scores the user: GREEN (safe), YELLOW (watch), RED (likely scammer/predator). Score updates in real time.6Temporary Ban + Admin AlertRED users are automatically temporarily banned. Admin team receives full conversation log + AI analysis for human review. Admin confirms or reverses.7Video Verification GateAll users — even GREEN — must pass video verification to exit quarantine. Live video call with AI face matching confirms real identity.8Permanent Ban for Confirmed Bad ActorsAdmin-confirmed scammers are permanently banned. Their face, voice, and behavioral fingerprint are stored. If they return with new email/number, our DeepFake AI identifies and bans them again.9Trust Score Shown PubliclyEvery verified user gets a visible Trust Score on their profile — Green Verified, Watch Flag, or Clean Record. Other users can see this.What the Safety AI DetectsSCAMMER SIGNALSDETECTION METHODCopy-paste romance scriptsText similarity AI — same messages across multiple conversationsPhone number / WhatsApp requests too earlyKeyword + timing pattern analysisFake emergency money requestsFinancial keyword detection + conversation flow analysisGrooming language patternsNLP trained on known grooming conversation datasetsFake profile (stolen photos)Reverse image search + face uniqueness scoringAggressive sexual languageContent moderation AI — instant flagEmotional manipulation tacticsSentiment escalation pattern detectionMultiple account creationDevice fingerprint + face matching across accountsThe 20 Decoy Profiles — Management20 volunteer test users create real-looking profiles across diverse demographicsTheir profiles are managed by the admin team — real photos, real bios, realistic personasThe Safety AI responds on their behalf — volunteers do NOT need to be activeDecoy profiles refreshed quarterly to keep them realistic and currentAdmin team monitors all Fish Trap conversations dailyFish Trap results feed back to improve AI detection accuracy continuously4. SUYAMVARAM — MATRIMONY MODESUYAMVARAMChoose your partner the ancient way — but powered by modern AI. BOND brings this back for modern independent adults. Any gender can post a Suyamvaram challenge. You define what you want. Participants prove they have it.Suyamvaram — Complete Flow1Challenge Creator Posts SuyamvaramPremium Matrimony user creates a challenge post. TRIBE ZONE — BOTH MODES🏕️  TRIBE ZONEFind someone who lives like you — not just looks like you.The Problem With Normal FiltersEvery dating and matrimony app filters by age, height, religion, caste, income.
