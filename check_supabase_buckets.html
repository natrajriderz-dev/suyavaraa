Checking Supabase storage buckets...

Required Buckets for Suyavaraa App:

  • avatars - For user profile photos (should be public)
  • posts - For social media posts (should be public)
  • verification_media - For video verification (can be private)

How to create missing buckets:

  1. Go to your Supabase Dashboard
  2. Click on "Storage" in the left menu
  3. Click "Create new bucket"
  4. Enter the bucket name exactly as shown above
  5. Set to "Public" for avatars and posts
  6. Click "Create bucket"
